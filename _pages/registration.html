Society for Mathematical Biology Annual Meeting - 2023

Celebrating SMB's 50th Anniversary at The Ohio State University (Columbus, Ohio)




Registration

Those wanting to register should email smb2023@osu.edu with the subject CONFERENCE REGISTRATION all in caps.




Registration costs:

Registration type Cost
Regular registration $650
Regular member registration $550
Student registration $550
Student member registration $450

** Early bird registration has now ended.

Excursions:All three of the excursions have reached capacity. There is the opportunity to add your name to the waitlist at registration.

Dormitory housing: Dormitory housing has reached capacity.




Refund requests:

Deadline to request a refund is July 1. If you are no longer able to attend the meeting, please email smb2023@osu.edu as soon as possible and no later than July 1 to request a refund. Requests received near the July 1 deadline may not be processed until after the meeting ends.
